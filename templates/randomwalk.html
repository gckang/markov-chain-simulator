Random Walk

A random walk typically consists of our transition states being some set S = {0, 1, 2,..., N} where our object moves either left or right. Formally from a state K an object can move to either K + 1 or K - 1. One of the main questions of the random walk is how long it takes for us to reach the boundary and we explore that with this example.
